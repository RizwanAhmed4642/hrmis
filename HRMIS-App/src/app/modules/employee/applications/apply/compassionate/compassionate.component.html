Transfer on Compassionate Grounds
Major Disability

In case of major disability, the applicant must upload disability certificate. For the first application, no merit will be applicable. The applicant can avail this facility only once and afterwards open merit will be applied.

Minor Disability

In case of minor disability, the applicant will get 10 additional marks. The applicant can avail this facility only once and afterwards open merit will be applied.

Wedlock as basis for transfer on compassionate grounds can be availed by MOs/WMOs multiple times, only if spouse is a government servant

  • The applicant will receive 10 additional marks under wedlock
  • For the first application for transfer on wedlock basis, the applicant must have served for at least one year in the current health facility. Thereafter, the applicant can apply for transfer under wedlock without completing one year tenure in one health facility
  • Employment letter issued by respective department of the spouse along with copy of last pay slip is required to submit application under wedlock

Divorce as basis for transfer on compassionate grounds can be availed only by WMOs and only in case of no re-marriage

  • The applicant will receive 15 additional marks for the first application submitted under divorce
  • Copies of Nikah Nama, divorce certificate and no re-marriage affidavit must be submitted by the applicant at the time of submitting the application
  • This facility can be availed only once, and afterwards open merit will be applied

Widow as basis for transfer on compassionate grounds can be availed only by WMOs and only in case of no re-marriage

  • The applicant will receive 10 additional marks for the first application submitted submitted under divorce
  • Copies of Nikah Nama, husband’s death certificate and no re-marriage affidavit must be submitted by the applicant
  • This facility can be availed only once, and afterwards open merit will be applied
